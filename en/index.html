ABOUT US

Our story begins when guns.ge, first themed forum, was founded back in 2005. By 2016, strong community of gun and precision rifle people was there to unite under “Georgian Shooters Union” which successfully hold several nationwide tournaments. As a result of increasing interest and engagement throughout past years, we become members of “International Precision Rifle Federation” and under its umbrella, continue to eagerly improve Georgia’s gun ownership and usage culture. Safety rules, precision and practical shooting types are all the areas we continuously improve and popularize. Our aim is to develop this sport, level up and represent Federation membership as from one of the highest performing Country.
